# The Utility Trap

<div grid="~ cols-2 gap-6">
<div>

## The Paradox

The strategy that wins **market position** is the same strategy that destroys the **equity premium**

- A utility wins by being **essential**
- A growth stock wins by being **exciting**

AWS is becoming the most essential infrastructure company in AI

**The market is punishing it for exactly that**

</div>
<div>

## Stock Performance

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| AMZN YTD 2026 | **-9%** |
| S&P 500 YTD | ~flat |
| AMZN 12-month | **-1%** |
| S&P 500 12-month | **+16%** |

Historical precedent: IBM, Cisco spent years at utility multiples before recovery
